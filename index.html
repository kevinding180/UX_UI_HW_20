My UX Skills

User-Centered Design

A UX designer should have a deep understanding of user behavior and be able to apply that understanding to create user-centered designs that are intuitive, accessible, and easy to use. This means conducting user research, creating personas and user journeys, and testing designs to ensure they meet the needs of users.

Interaction Design

A UX designer should have strong skills in interaction design, which is the design of the interactions between users and digital products. This requires a deep understanding of human-computer interaction principles and the ability to design interfaces that are intuitive and engaging for users. A good interaction designer can create interfaces that are easy to use, and are accessible to people with disabilities.

Visual Design

Visual design is an essential component of UX design and is concerned with the look and feel of digital products. A UX designer with strong visual design skills is able to create aesthetically pleasing interfaces that are both functional and attractive. This includes creating wireframes, prototypes, and visual design comps that demonstrate how a product will look and function. Good visual design skills also include a solid understanding of color theory, typography, and layout principles.

My Work

Fit Match

As a part of a UX design team, I played a key role in the development of FitMatch - a cutting-edge mobile application that streamlines the process of putting together stylish and weather-appropriate outfits. The app addresses the common challenge of busy schedules and unpredictable weather, making it difficult for individuals to plan their outfits.With the implementation of real-time weather forecasts and on-demand stylist services, FitMatch transforms the experience of getting dressed into an enjoyable and interactive one. Our team employed user-centered design principles and a user-friendly interface to create an intuitive and easy-to-use app that helps users effortlessly coordinate outfits that are both fashionable and weather-suitable.

PAWS Redesign

In this UX project, the redesign of the animal welfare society website (https://www.pawsweb.org/index.html) was undertaken to improve the user experience. Through user research and testing, key pain points in the existing site were identified, and a redesign strategy was developed. User-centered design principles were utilized to create a modern and intuitive interface that improved navigation, increased accessibility, and made it easier for users to find the information they needed. The new design included a responsive layout and clear, concise information architecture. The redesign resulted in improved engagement and satisfaction from the website visitors.

FCC Redesign

A redesign of the Federal Communications Commission (FCC) government website was undertaken to improve the user experience. Research and testing identified key pain points in the existing site, and a redesign strategy was developed. User-centered design principles were utilized to create a modern and intuitive interface. Navigation, accessibility, and the ability to find information were improved. A responsive layout and clear, concise information architecture were implemented. As a result, engagement and satisfaction were improved.
